Who are we?

We are like totally comedy!

We like people who make us laugh, and we like making people laugh. We are a comedy co-operative of comedy performers who collectively believe that there needs to be a shake-up of comedy; new experiences which embrace the chaotic nature of live performance, and which welcomes a new generation of comedy connoisseurs from the young generation. We mostly believe that this new-fangled menagerie of millennials, mostly, do not know what-on-earth live comedy is. They turn up at comedy clubs expecting Live at the Apollo and end up disappointed when the night isn’t a slickly edited and highly polished snapshot of stand-up (and they are often confused when to laugh without regular cutaways to the resident celebrity laughing).

Enter us! Totally Comedy! Saviours of the world! The alternative comedy revolution faded many years ago, and the circuit is filled with misogynist mother in-law jokes and ‘original’ material about how terrific tinder is. We embrace the different, the unusual, and the playful. Do you love live comedy? Then join us at our anarchic mash-up of everything not-of-the-norm at Clusterf**k, our resident monthly night at The Miller with our good pals ‘Best Boy’.

Yours graciously,

Jiid Jasper

Founder(ish)
